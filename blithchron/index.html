About Us

To students in Ahmedabad and Gandinagar, Blithchron was more than just a fest, it was the dawning of an era.The idea to embark on this journey was envisioned as a thought being unique. The same thought process translated into creating events that were groundbreaking in their own domain.Events like 'The Butler Did It' , 'Jobless' have continued to leave the audience, participants and media awestruck at the ingenuity of the students. Blithchron has been the host to some of the biggest rock bands in India: Agnee, Motherjane and Parikrama, and with the introduction of a cultural night, we have made sure that Blithchron is a festival that appeals to diverse people across Gujarat. At Blithchron, we have continuously endeavored to make sure that the people who attend the fest are mesmerized by the quality, standard and the stature of our events.

So come one and all at IIT Gandinagar to witness two days of fun and frolic.
